The menu

We, the kitchen staff of American Steakhouse have created a menu to get the utmost flavor out of the meat. From steaks to seafood there is always an option for your liking! Every dish comes with an option from the sidedished section of the menu. Any allergies or preferences can be taken into consideration for your dish. We that we can prepare a meal for you soon!
