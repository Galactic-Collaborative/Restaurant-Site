Alcoholic beverages

With your delicious meal you of course want the drink to be as delicious as the food. Because of this we have selected a range of beverages just for you! Whether you're a wine or a beer person, we have a beverage for you! Josiah has personally selected a range of craft beers for local breweries or you can enjoy the ice cold house beer. If wine is more your kind of drink, we also have an amazing selection of wines from vineyards we have selected with the upmost care.

If you have any questions about the beers or the wine you can just ask our beer expert and sommelier when you come for diner!
