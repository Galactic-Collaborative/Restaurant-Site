History

The American Steakhouse company has existed for nearly a century. During these years, we have experienced quite a lot of things. Please join us as we navigate through our history, starting with Josiah Goodmen's founding of the American Steakhouse.

When Josiah was a young kid, he would go out with his father to explore the nearby Yellowstone park. This started his passion for the park. When his father was asked to join the army and was deployed to fight in the great war, he often spend time in the park alone.

One day, when he was walking with a group of friends, he got lost and could not find his way back on one of the main paths. Josiah fought for his live in the wilderness, using his knife to kill for food. It was this moment, Josiah realized the meat he hunted was actually really tasty!

After a couple of days, Josiah saw the interstate from a mountain top nearby and walked towards it. A stranger generously lifted him back to his home in Jackson where Josiah immediately started working on a business.

It started from his grandma's basement, which is a huge downgrade compared to Josiah's current restaurant Josiah was allowed to use the kitchen, which he used to full potential. On the opening day, before going to the market to buy fresh meat, he submitted an ad in a local newspaper. Because of the Roaring Twenties, the advertisement was no mistake: the basement was full in no time. This encouraged Josiah to expand his business.

He started his search for the ideal place. Josiah knew it had to be somewhere, where it felt home. As Josiah was a bit of an adventurer, he chose a place near the Yellowstone National Park. Josiah did fear that being too far out of town could interfere with his business, but he was proven wrong three weeks later, when the establishment we are still using today, was built.

In the legacy of Josiah we are always looking to expand our business and searching for the best possible ingredients for our dishes. As Josiah himself searched the continents and the worlds oceans to get the meat and seafood he wants to serve, we will continue to do the same. While also searching for the opportunity to bring our restaurants to you so you can enjoy your steak on the beach somewhere!
